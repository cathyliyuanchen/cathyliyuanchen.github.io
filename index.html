About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Chicago, mentored by Prof. Ben Zhao and Prof. Heather Zheng at SAND Lab (Security, Algorithms, Networking and Data). My research interests are broadly at the intersection of ML, security, and privacy. I am also interested in what we can learn about the fundamentals of ML from efforts to make deep learning more secure and robust.
Previously, I was an AI Resident at FAIR working on cryptanalysis with ML, mentored by Kristin Lauter and François Charton. Before that, I did ML research at Real-time Intelligent Secure Explainable systems Lab (RISELab), mentored by Joseph Gonzalez.
